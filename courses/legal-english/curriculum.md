# 法律英语词汇 12 周课程大纲

## 课程总览

本课程为中文母语者设计，聚焦商务合同法律英语。每周7天：1天词汇预载 + 5天新课 + 1天复习。

---

**目标**：掌握合同的基本组成部分和相关核心术语

| 天 | 内容 |
|----|------|
| 1 | **词汇预载**：本周术语一键添加到背单词App |
| 2 | 合同总体结构：title, preamble, recitals, definitions, operative provisions, boilerplate, schedules |
| 3 | 合同当事人：party, hereinafter, whereas, witnesseth, now therefore, enter into, by and between |
| 4 | 定义条款：shall mean, as used herein, material, affiliate, person, including without limitation, hereof/thereof |
| 5 | 合同标的与履行义务：subject matter, deliverables, performance, due diligence, scope of work, milestone |
| 6 | 合同生效与期限：effective date, term, commencement, expiration, renewal, termination |
| 7 | **复习日**：第1周内容综合练习 |
