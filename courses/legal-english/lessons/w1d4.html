第1周 第4天 — 定义条款（Definitions Clause）

学习目标：掌握合同定义条款中的核心词汇和固定搭配，理解 shall mean、as used herein、including without limitation 等法律表达的精确含义，学会阅读和起草合同定义条款。

上节回顾：第2天学习了合同当事人条款（party, hereinafter, whereas, witnesseth, now therefore）和过渡条款的标准格式。今天进入合同正文的第一个核心板块——定义条款（Definitions），它决定了整份合同中关键术语的确切含义。

⚠️ 多义词辨析：material

  • 法律英语：重大的；实质性的（形容词）——这是合同中最常见的含义

3. 词汇辨析

3a. hereof vs. thereof vs. herein vs. therein

术语中文含义拆解
hereof 本协议的 here（本协议）+ of（的）= of this Agreement
例：Section 3 hereof = 本协议第3条
thereof 其……的 there（该事物）+ of（的）= of that / of it
例：the terms and conditions thereof = 其条款和条件
herein 在本协议中 here（本协议）+ in（在……中）= in this Agreement
例：as used herein = 如本协议所使用的
therein 在其中 there（该事物）+ in（在……中）= in that / in it
例：the obligations contained therein = 其中所含的义务
常见翻译错误：
  • hereof 不要译为"在此之上"或"在此之前"，正确翻译为"本协议的"
  • thereof 不要与 hereof 混淆——hereof 指本协议，thereof 指前文提到的其他事物

3b. shall mean vs. means vs. refers to

术语中文使用场景
shall mean 系指；应当指 最正式，合同定义条款的标准用法。shall 表示确定性和约束力。
means 指；意为 较现代的写法，越来越多的合同采用。与 shall mean 含义相同但更简洁。
refers to 指的是；涉及 较少在定义条款中使用，更多出现在解释性说明中。不如 shall mean 正式。

3c. including without limitation vs. including but not limited to vs. such as

术语中文区别
including without limitation 包括但不限于 最常用的合同表达，明确表示列举为示例性而非穷尽性。
including but not limited to 包括但不限于 与上一个含义完全相同，更口语化。两者可互换。
such as 例如；如 较口语化，在正式合同中较少使用。可能被法院解读为穷尽性列举。

4. 例句与语境

定义条款引导语

1. As used herein, the following terms shall have the following meanings, unless the context otherwise requires: — 如本协议所使用的，除非上下文另有要求，以下术语具有以下含义：

2. Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Section shall have the meanings ascribed to them elsewhere in this Agreement. — 本条中使用但未定义的首字母大写术语，具有本协议其他条款中赋予其的含义。

具体定义条款

3. "Affiliate" shall mean, with respect to any Person, any other Person that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with such Person. — "关联方"是指就任何人士而言，直接或间接控制该人士、受该人士控制或与该人士受共同控制的任何其他人士。

4. "Business Day" shall mean any day other than a Saturday, Sunday, or any day on which commercial banks in New York City are authorized or required by law to close. — "工作日"是指除星期六、星期日或纽约市商业银行依法被授权或被要求关闭营业的任何日期以外的任何日期。

5. "Confidential Information" shall mean all non-public information disclosed by one party to the other, whether orally, in writing, or in any other form, including without limitation trade secrets, business plans, financial data, and customer lists. — "保密信息"是指一方向另一方披露的所有非公开信息，无论以口头、书面或其他任何形式披露，包括但不限于商业秘密、商业计划、财务数据和客户名单。

6. "Material Adverse Effect" shall mean any event, change, or occurrence that, individually or in the aggregate, has had or would reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect on the business, financial condition, or results of operations of the Company. — "重大不利影响"是指任何单独或合计已经对公司的业务、财务状况或经营成果产生或可合理预期将产生重大不利影响的事件、变化或事项。

7. "Person" shall mean any individual, corporation, limited liability company, partnership, joint venture, trust, unincorporated organization, governmental authority, or any other entity of any kind. — "人士"是指任何个人、公司、有限责任公司、合伙企业、合营企业、信托、非法人组织、政府机构或任何其他任何类型的实体。

交叉引用与限定

8. "Effective Date" shall have the meaning set forth in Section 12.1 hereof. — "生效日期"具有本协议第12.1条中载明的含义。

9. The term "Losses" shall include, without limitation, any and all damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses, including reasonable attorneys' fees and court costs. — "损失"一词包括但不限于任何及所有损害赔偿、责任、费用和开支，包括合理的律师费和诉讼费用。

10. Any reference herein to a "Section" or "Article" shall refer to a section or article hereof, and any reference to a "Schedule" shall refer to a schedule attached hereto. — 本协议中对"条"或"章"的任何引用均指本协议的条或章，对"附表"的任何引用均指本协议所附的附表。

💡 here- / there- / where- 家族总结：
  • hereof = of this (Agreement)（本协议的）
  • herein = in this (Agreement)（在本协议中）
  • hereto = to this (Agreement)（对本协议；本协议所附的）
  • thereof = of that / of it（其……的）
  • therein = in that / in it（在其中）
  • thereto = to that / to it（对其）
  • whereby = by which（据此；凭借）

这些词在合同中替代了重复使用合同名称或先行词，使语言更简洁、更正式。

5. 长难句拆解

长难句 1

"Affiliate" shall mean, with respect to any Person, any other Person that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with such Person, where "control" means the possession, directly or indirectly, of the power to direct or cause the direction of the management and policies of a Person, whether through the ownership of voting securities, by contract, or otherwise.
  • 主干："Affiliate" shall mean ... any other Person（"关联方"是指……任何其他人士）
  • 限定范围：with respect to any Person（就任何人士而言）
  • 定语从句（三个并列关系）：that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with such Person（直接或间接控制该人士、受该人士控制或与该人士受共同控制的）
  • 补充定义"控制"：where "control" means the possession ... of the power to direct or cause the direction of the management and policies（其中"控制"是指拥有……指导或促使指导某人士的管理和政策的权力）
  • 控制的实现方式（三种并列）：whether through the ownership of voting securities, by contract, or otherwise（无论是通过持有有表决权的证券、通过合同或以其他方式）
翻译："关联方"是指就任何人士而言，直接或间接控制该人士、受该人士控制或与该人士受共同控制的任何其他人士，其中"控制"是指直接或间接拥有指导或促使指导某人士的管理和政策的权力，无论是通过持有有表决权的证券、通过合同还是以其他方式。

长难句 2

"Material Adverse Effect" shall mean any event, change, circumstance, or occurrence that, individually or in the aggregate, has had or would reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect on (a) the business, assets, financial condition, or results of operations of the Company and its Subsidiaries, taken as a whole, or (b) the ability of the Seller to consummate the transactions contemplated by this Agreement.
  • 主干："Material Adverse Effect" shall mean any event, change, circumstance, or occurrence（"重大不利影响"是指任何事件、变化、情况或事项）
  • 定语从句主干：that ... has had or would reasonably be expected to have a material adverse effect on...（已经产生或可合理预期将对……产生重大不利影响的）
  • 插入语：individually or in the aggregate（单独或合计）
  • 影响范围 (a)：the business, assets, financial condition, or results of operations of the Company and its Subsidiaries, taken as a whole（公司及其子公司整体的业务、资产、财务状况或经营成果）
  • 影响范围 (b)：the ability of the Seller to consummate the transactions contemplated by this Agreement（卖方完成本协议拟进行的交易的能力）
  • 关键词：consummate = 完成/达成（交易）；contemplated by = 拟进行的/预期的
翻译："重大不利影响"是指任何单独或合计已经对以下事项产生或可合理预期将产生重大不利影响的事件、变化、情况或事项：(a) 公司及其子公司整体的业务、资产、财务状况或经营成果；或 (b) 卖方完成本协议拟进行的交易的能力。

长难句 3

Unless the context otherwise requires, (i) words importing the singular include the plural and vice versa, (ii) references to any statute or statutory provision shall include any subordinate legislation made under it, and (iii) any reference to a "day" shall mean a calendar day unless specifically stated to be a Business Day.
  • 条件状语：Unless the context otherwise requires（除非上下文另有要求）
  • 规则 (i)：words importing the singular include the plural and vice versa（表示单数的词语包含复数含义，反之亦然）
  • 规则 (ii)：references to any statute or statutory provision shall include any subordinate legislation made under it（对任何法规或法律条文的引用均包含据其制定的附属立法）
  • 规则 (iii)：any reference to a "day" shall mean a calendar day unless specifically stated to be a Business Day（对"日"的任何引用均指日历日，除非明确规定为工作日）
  • 关键词：importing = 含有……意义的；subordinate legislation = 附属立法/下位法；vice versa = 反之亦然
翻译：除非上下文另有要求：(i) 表示单数的词语包含复数含义，反之亦然；(ii) 对任何法规或法律条文的引用均包含据其制定的附属立法；(iii) 对"日"的任何引用均指日历日，除非明确规定为工作日。

8. 今日小结

  1. shall mean——定义条款的标准引导词，"X" shall mean... = "X"是指……
  2. shall have the meaning ascribed to it in——交叉引用定义的标准表达
  3. as used herein——限定术语适用范围为本协议
  4. unless the context otherwise requires——灵活性限定语，为定义留出解释空间
  5. affiliate——关联方（控制/受控制/共同控制关系）
  6. person（法律定义）——范围涵盖自然人和一切法人实体
  7. material——重大的、实质性的（非"物质的"），material breach = 重大违约
  8. including without limitation——包括但不限于，表示示例性而非穷尽性列举
  9. hereof / thereof——here- 和 there- 家族词，分别指代本协议和前文提到的事物
  10. set forth——载明、规定，as set forth in = 如……所载明的
  11. ascribe——赋予（含义），常与 meaning 搭配使用
  12. effective date / business day——合同中必定出现的基础定义术语
