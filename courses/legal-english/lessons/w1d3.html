第1周 第3天 — 合同当事人（Parties to a Contract）

学习目标：掌握合同中描述当事人身份、引出背景和过渡到正式条款的核心术语，理解 whereas/witnesseth/now therefore 等法律英语特有表达的功能和用法。

上节回顾：第1天学习了合同的七大组成部分（title, preamble, recitals, definitions, operative provisions, boilerplate, schedules）。今天聚焦合同开头段落——当事人条款和引导语。

2. 固定搭配

以下是合同当事人条款中最常见的固定搭配，这些表达在真实合同中高频出现，建议整体记忆。

duly organized and existing under the laws of 依……法律合法设立并存续 ABC Inc., a corporation duly organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware.
hereinafter referred to as 以下简称为 XYZ Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer") agrees to the terms hereof.

3. 词汇辨析

3a. enter into vs. sign vs. execute

术语中文使用场景
enter into 签订；订立 指缔结合同关系，强调法律行为本身，最正式。
搭配：enter into an agreement / a contract
sign 签字；签署 侧重物理签字动作，较口语化。
搭配：sign a document / sign on the dotted line
execute 签署；执行 极正式，同时含"签署"和"使生效"之意。
搭配：execute and deliver this Agreement
常见翻译错误：
  • "签合同"不要译为 sign a contract（偏口语），合同中应用 enter into an agreement 或 execute this Agreement

3b. whereas vs. now therefore vs. witnesseth

术语中文功能
whereas 鉴于 引出合同背景事实，位于 recitals（鉴于条款）部分。每条 WHEREAS 陈述一项背景。
now therefore 因此，现 过渡语，衔接 recitals 与 operative provisions（正式条款）。表示"基于上述背景，双方同意如下"。
witnesseth 兹证明 古英语形式，出现在合同开头或过渡处，表示"本协议特此证明"。现代合同中较少使用。

3c. party vs. person vs. entity

术语中文区别
party 当事人；缔约方 仅指合同签约方，范围最窄。the parties hereto = 本协议各方。
person 人士 法律定义中范围最广，含自然人、公司、合伙企业、信托等一切法律主体。
entity 实体 通常指非自然人的组织体（公司、合伙等），不含自然人。

4. 例句与语境

合同开头——当事人介绍

1. This Agreement is made and entered into as of January 15, 2026, by and between ABC Corporation, a company duly organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware (hereinafter referred to as "the Seller"), and XYZ Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of England and Wales (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer"). — 本协议由ABC公司（一家依特拉华州法律合法设立并存续的公司，主要营业地位于特拉华州威尔明顿市梅因街100号，以下简称"卖方"）与XYZ有限公司（一家依英格兰和威尔士法律设立的公司，以下简称"买方"）于2026年1月15日签订。

2. Each party represents that it has the full power and authority to enter into this Agreement. — 各方声明其拥有签订本协议的完全权力和授权。

3. No third party shall have any rights or benefits under this Agreement. — 任何第三方不得享有本协议项下的任何权利或利益。

鉴于条款（Whereas Clauses / Recitals）

4. WHEREAS, the Seller is engaged in the business of manufacturing and distributing industrial equipment; — 鉴于，卖方从事工业设备的制造和经销业务；

5. WHEREAS, the Buyer desires to purchase certain equipment from the Seller on the terms and conditions set forth herein; — 鉴于，买方希望按本协议所列条款和条件从卖方购买特定设备；

6. WHEREAS, the parties have conducted arm's length negotiations and wish to memorialize their understanding in this Agreement; — 鉴于，双方经过公平独立的谈判，希望将其共识记载于本协议中；

过渡条款（Transitional Clause）

7. NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows: — 因此，基于本协议所载的相互约定和承诺，以及其他充分有效的对价（双方特此确认已收到该等对价且对价充分），双方协议如下：

8. This Agreement witnesseth that the Licensor hereby grants to the Licensee a non-exclusive license. — 本协议兹证明，许可方特此授予被许可方一项非排他性许可。

当事人身份描述

9. The Contractor, a limited liability company duly organized under the laws of the People's Republic of China, with its registered address at Pudong New Area, Shanghai. — 承包方，一家依中华人民共和国法律合法设立的有限责任公司，注册地址位于上海市浦东新区。

10. The parties hereto have caused this Agreement to be executed by their duly authorized representatives. — 本协议各方已由其正式授权的代表签署本协议。

11. Hereinafter, ABC Corporation and XYZ Ltd. shall be collectively referred to as "the Parties" and individually as "a Party". — 以下将ABC公司和XYZ有限公司合称为"双方"，单独称为"一方"。

💡 搭配提示：hereinafter referred to as 是合同中引入简称的标准表达。注意 hereinafter 仅出现在首次定义简称时，后续条款直接使用简称即可。

5. 长难句拆解

长难句 1

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:
  • 主干：the parties agree as follows（双方协议如下）
  • 原因状语①：in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained（基于本协议所载的相互约定和承诺）
  • 原因状语②：and for other good and valuable consideration（以及其他充分有效的对价）
  • 非限定性定语从句：the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged（双方特此确认已收到该等对价且对价充分）——修饰 consideration
  • 过渡词：NOW, THEREFORE（因此，现）——衔接前面的 WHEREAS 鉴于条款，引出正式协议条款
翻译：因此，基于本协议所载的相互约定和承诺，以及其他充分有效的对价（双方特此确认已收到该等对价且对价充分），双方协议如下：

长难句 2

This Agreement is made and entered into as of the 15th day of January, 2026, by and between ABC Corporation, a company duly organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 100 Main Street, Wilmington, Delaware (hereinafter referred to as "the Seller"), and XYZ Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of England and Wales (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer").
  • 主干：This Agreement is made and entered into（本协议签订）
  • 时间状语：as of the 15th day of January, 2026（于2026年1月15日）
  • 当事人①：by and between ABC Corporation（由ABC公司）
  • 同位语①：a company duly organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware（一家依特拉华州法律合法设立并存续的公司）
  • 介词短语：with its principal place of business at 100 Main Street...（主要营业地位于……）
  • 插入语：(hereinafter referred to as "the Seller")（以下简称"卖方"）
  • 当事人②：and XYZ Ltd.（与XYZ有限公司）
  • 同位语②：a company incorporated under the laws of England and Wales（一家依英格兰和威尔士法律设立的公司）
翻译：本协议由ABC公司（一家依特拉华州法律合法设立并存续的公司，主要营业地位于特拉华州威尔明顿市梅因街100号，以下简称"卖方"）与XYZ有限公司（一家依英格兰和威尔士法律设立的公司，以下简称"买方"）于2026年1月15日签订。

长难句 3

WHEREAS, the parties have conducted arm's length negotiations and wish to memorialize their understanding in this Agreement, and WHEREAS, each party represents and warrants that it has the requisite corporate power and authority to enter into, execute, and deliver this Agreement;
  • 鉴于条款①主干：the parties have conducted arm's length negotiations and wish to memorialize their understanding（双方经过公平独立的谈判，希望将其共识记载于本协议）
  • 关键搭配：arm's length negotiations = 公平独立的谈判（无关联关系的双方之间的谈判）
  • 关键搭配：memorialize = 记载；以书面形式记录
  • 鉴于条款②主干：each party represents and warrants that...（各方声明并保证……）
  • 宾语从句：it has the requisite corporate power and authority to enter into, execute, and deliver this Agreement（其拥有签订、签署和交付本协议所需的公司权力和授权）
翻译：鉴于，双方经过公平独立的谈判，希望将其共识记载于本协议中；并鉴于，各方声明并保证其拥有签订、签署和交付本协议所需的公司权力和授权；

8. 今日小结

  1. party（当事人）——合同中最基础的概念，the parties hereto = 本协议各方
  2. hereinafter referred to as——引入简称的标准搭配，仅在首次出现时使用
  3. whereas——鉴于条款的引导词，用于陈述签约背景和事实
  4. witnesseth——古英语形式的"证明"，-eth = 第三人称单数词尾
  5. now therefore——从鉴于条款过渡到正式条款的衔接语
  6. enter into——签订合同的标准动词搭配（非 sign，sign 仅指物理签字动作）
  7. by and between——介绍双方当事人的固定格式
  8. duly organized and existing under the laws of——描述公司合法设立并存续的固定搭配
  9. in consideration of——基于……的约因/对价（consideration = 约因，合同法核心概念）
  10. the premises——法律用语中指"上述事项"而非"房屋"
  11. arm's length——公平独立的（非关联方之间的交易/谈判）
