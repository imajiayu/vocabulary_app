第1周 第2天 — 合同总体结构

学习目标：掌握英文商务合同的总体结构框架，识别并理解合同各组成部分的英文名称及其功能，为后续逐层深入学习奠定基础。

1. 核心词汇

英文商务合同的结构高度标准化。无论是股权收购协议、服务合同还是保密协议，其骨架基本一致。以下 12 个术语构成了合同结构的核心词汇。

英文音标中文常见搭配
title /ˈtaɪtl/ 合同标题；所有权 contract title; title of the agreement; title page
preamble /priːˈæmbəl/ 序文；前言 the preamble of this Agreement; preamble clause; as set forth in the preamble
recitals /rɪˈsaɪtəlz/ 鉴于条款；背景叙述 the recitals hereto; recitals section; as described in the recitals
definitions /ˌdefɪˈnɪʃənz/ 定义条款 definitions and interpretation; as defined in Article 1; defined terms
operative provisions /ˈɒpərətɪv prəˈvɪʒənz/ 实质性条款；正文条款 the operative provisions of this Agreement; key operative provisions
boilerplate /ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/ 通用条款；标准条款 boilerplate clauses; boilerplate provisions; standard boilerplate language
schedules /ˈʃedjuːlz/ 附件；附表 schedules attached hereto; as set out in Schedule A; schedule of deliverables
clause /klɔːz/ 条款 termination clause; penalty clause; pursuant to Clause 5.2
provision /prəˈvɪʒən/ 规定；条款 general provisions; subject to the provisions of; key provisions
article /ˈɑːrtɪkəl/ 条；章 Article 3 of this Agreement; under this Article; article by article
amendment /əˈmendmənt/ 修正案；修订 amendment to the Agreement; first amendment; amendment and restatement
execution /ˌeksɪˈkjuːʃən/ 签署；执行 execution of this Agreement; execution copy; upon execution
辨析：clause vs. provision vs. article
三者均可译为"条款"，但使用场景有别。Article 通常指合同的大章节编号（如 Article 1, Article 2），层级最高。Clause 指具体的条文，偏口语化，也常用于指某一特定条款（如 termination clause）。Provision 最为通用，既可指单条条款，也可泛指一组条款（如 general provisions），语域最正式。

2. 例句与语境

2.1 合同标题与序言

1. This Share Purchase Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026. — 本《股权收购协议》（以下简称"本协议"）于2026年1月15日签订。

2. The preamble identifies the parties to this Agreement and the date of execution. — 序文载明本协议的当事人及签署日期。

3. The recitals set forth the background and purpose of the transaction contemplated herein. — 鉴于条款阐述了本协议所述交易的背景和目的。

4. Unless otherwise defined herein, capitalized terms used in this Agreement shall have the meanings ascribed to them in Article 1 (Definitions). — 除本协议另有定义外，本协议中使用的大写术语应具有第一条（定义）中赋予的含义。

2.2 合同正文与结构

5. The operative provisions of this Agreement shall become effective upon execution by both parties. — 本协议的实质性条款自双方签署后生效。

6. Each article of this Agreement addresses a distinct aspect of the parties' rights and obligations. — 本协议各条分别针对当事人权利义务的不同方面。

7. The Seller's obligations under Clause 4.2 are subject to the conditions set forth in Article 6. — 卖方在第4.2条项下的义务须受第六条所列条件的约束。

8. Any amendment to this Agreement shall be in writing and duly executed by all parties hereto. — 对本协议的任何修订均须以书面形式作出，并经全体当事人正式签署。

2.3 通用条款与附件

9. The boilerplate provisions, including severability and entire agreement clauses, are contained in Article 12. — 通用条款，包括可分割性条款和完整协议条款，载于第十二条。

10. The schedules attached hereto shall form an integral part of this Agreement. — 本协议所附附件构成本协议不可分割的组成部分。

11. The provisions of this Agreement shall survive the execution and delivery of the closing documents. — 本协议之条款在交割文件签署交付后继续有效。

12. This Agreement has been executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original. — 本协议一式两份签署，每份均视为正本。

3. 长难句拆解

长难句 1

Unless otherwise defined herein, capitalized terms used in this Agreement shall have the meanings ascribed to them in Article 1 (Definitions), and any reference to a "Clause" or "Article" shall be construed as a reference to a clause or article of this Agreement.
  • 条件状语：Unless otherwise defined herein — 除非本协议另有定义
  • 主句1：capitalized terms ... shall have the meanings ascribed to them in Article 1 — 大写术语具有第一条中的含义
  • 并列主句2：any reference to a "Clause" or "Article" shall be construed as ... — 对"条款"或"条"的提及应解释为……
  • 关键搭配：ascribed to（赋予）；construed as（解释为）
翻译：除本协议另有定义外，本协议中使用的大写术语应具有第一条（定义）中赋予其的含义，且对"条款"或"条"的任何引用均应被解释为对本协议中相应条款或条的引用。

长难句 2

The Schedules and Exhibits attached hereto and referred to herein are hereby incorporated in and made a part of this Agreement with the same force and effect as if set forth in full in the body hereof.
  • 主语：The Schedules and Exhibits — 附件和附录
  • 后置定语1：attached hereto — 附于本协议的
  • 后置定语2：and referred to herein — 并在本协议中提及的
  • 谓语：are hereby incorporated in and made a part of this Agreement — 特此纳入本协议并成为其组成部分
  • 状语：with the same force and effect as if set forth in full in the body hereof — 具有与在本协议正文中全文载入同等的效力
  • 关键搭配：attached hereto（附于本协议）；incorporated in（纳入）；with the same force and effect（具有同等效力）
翻译：附于本协议并在本协议中提及的各附件和附录，特此纳入本协议并构成其组成部分，与在本协议正文中全文载入具有同等效力。

长难句 3

No amendment, modification, or waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective unless it is in writing and signed by the party against whom enforcement of such amendment, modification, or waiver is sought.
  • 主语：No amendment, modification, or waiver of any provision of this Agreement — 对本协议任何条款的修订、修改或弃权
  • 谓语：shall be effective — 不具有效力
  • 条件状语：unless it is in writing and signed by the party — 除非以书面形式作出并经该方签署
  • 定语从句：against whom enforcement of such amendment, modification, or waiver is sought — 被请求执行该修订、修改或弃权的一方
  • 关键搭配：waiver of any provision（对条款的弃权）；enforcement is sought（请求执行）
翻译：对本协议任何条款的修订、修改或弃权，除非以书面形式作出并经被请求执行该修订、修改或弃权的一方签署，否则不具有效力。

6. 今日小结

  1. 合同七段式结构：Title → Preamble → Recitals → Definitions → Operative Provisions → Boilerplate → Schedules，这是英文商务合同的标准骨架。
  2. 三个"条款"辨析：article（大章节编号）＞ clause（具体条文）≈ provision（最通用的正式用语）。
  3. amendment 与 execution：amendment 指对已签合同的修订变更；execution 在合同语境中特指正式签署，而非日常语义的"执行"。
  4. boilerplate 不等于不重要：虽是"标准条款"，但管辖法律、争议解决、可分割性等条款直接影响合同的可执行性，实务中须逐条审查。
  5. schedules 的法律地位：附件经"incorporated herein"条款纳入后，与合同正文具有同等效力。
